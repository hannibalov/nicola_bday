SCAVENGER HUNT:
HOW TO PLAY

1
Recruitment

Join your 'Vice Squad' team.

Head to the registration desk to get your team color and dossier. Every squad needs a captain.

2
Intelligence

Track down the objects on the list.

The list is hidden around the Sunset Strip floor. Check behind palm trees and near the DJ booth.

3
Evidence

Snap a photo of each find to earn points.

No photo, no points. Upload your evidence directly to the Hunt tab in the app to climb the leaderboard.

The clock starts at midnight
